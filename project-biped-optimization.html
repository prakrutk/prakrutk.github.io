Gait and Trajectory Optimization for Bipeds

Status: Completed Tools: MATLAB, Python

Abstract

This project implements trajectory optimization on a 5-link kneed bipedal walker. Generated both periodic and non-periodic gaits for flat and uneven surfaces. Also developed understanding of non-linear dynamics and chaos theory for passive walking.

Key Components

  • 5-link biped model: With knees and point feet
  • Trajectory optimization: Using direct collocation methods
  • Passive walker: Two-link walker demonstrating chaotic dynamics
  • Stability analysis: Poincaré maps and basin of attraction

Results

Generated stable periodic gaits for walking speeds of 0.3-1.0 m/s. Demonstrated chaotic behavior in passive walkers and analyzed bifurcations in the gait family.
