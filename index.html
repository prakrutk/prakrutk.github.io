Hi, I'm Prakrut

PhD Student @ IISc Bangalore

I am a doctoral researcher at the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems (RBCCPS), Indian Institute of Science. I am advised by Prof. Shishir NY.

My research lies at the intersection of Robotics, Control Theory, and Machine Learning. Specifically, I am interested in:

  • Quadruped Locomotion & Dynamics
  • Physics-Guided World Models
  • Optimal Control (MPC)

Previously, I graduated from VNIT Nagpur and worked with IvLabs.
